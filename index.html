Our facial treatments are designed to rejuvenate and enhance your skin's natural glow. Whether it's Aqua Gold microneedling for a radiant complexion, TCA peel to achieve smoother skin, or dermaplaning for immediate softness, our treatments deliver impeccable results.

Patient Guidance

Pre & Post Treatment Instructions

Helpful care notes for injectables and dermal fillers. Open each item to review your before-and-after guidance.

Injectables — Pre Treatment +
  • Avoid intense cardiovascular exercise for 24 hours (cycling, running, HIIT classes, etc.).
  • Plan to wait 5 days before doing a facial or other skin treatments.
  • You can expect neurotoxin full effect from about 3 days up to 2 weeks.
Injectables — Post Treatment +

Mild swelling and bruising can occur after cosmetic injections and usually resolve quickly. Do not apply cosmetics or creams to any injection site for at least 12 hours.

  • For 72 hours before your visit, avoid alcohol, aspirin, ibuprofen, naproxen, CoQ10, and fish oil to reduce bruising risk.
  • To support recovery, sublingual Arnica or topical Arnica gel may be used before and after treatment.
Dermal Filler — Pre Treatment +
  • To support recovery, you may use sublingual Arnica or topical Arnica gel before treatment.
  • For 72 hours before your visit, avoid alcohol, aspirin, ibuprofen, naproxen, CoQ10, and fish oil to reduce bruising risk.
Dermal Filler — Post Treatment +
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications for 3 days.
  • Avoid alcohol for 3 days.
  • If swelling occurs, sleep with your head elevated for 2 to 3 days.
  • You may take acetaminophen (Tylenol) for mild tenderness or discomfort.
  • Wait at least 2 weeks before any skin care or laser treatments.
  • Bruising and swelling are expected; full effect and tissue integration usually take 2 to 3 weeks.
  • Arnica Montana may help minimize bruising (often started 1 week before injections).
  • Do not apply cleanser, moisturizer, or makeup until the day after treatment.
  • Do not massage, touch, or manipulate the injection site.
  • Ice may be used in the first 12 hours: 15 minutes each hour as needed.
  • Avoid heavy exercise the day of treatment.
  • Avoid dental work for 2 weeks after filler injections.
  • Avoid sleeping face down, on your side, or on your stomach for 7 to 10 days; sleep elevated on your back.

Contact the office immediately if you notice:

  • Sharp pain
  • Headache
  • Increasing warmth or coolness to touch
  • An unusual bruise or spreading bruise
  • Skin changes, severe swelling, or redness

These are general guidelines. Your personalized post-care plan may vary based on your treatment.
