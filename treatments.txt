PRE TREATMENT INSTRUCTIONS FOR INJECTABLES:
    •    Avoid intense cardiovascular exercise for 24 hours (i.e. cycling, running, HIIT classes, etc.)
    •    We recommend waiting 5 days before doing a facial or other skin treatments
    •    You should expect to see the neurotoxin’s full effect from 3 days up to 2 weeks
POST TREATMENT INSTRUCTIONS FOR INJECTABLES:
As with any injectable procedure, cosmetic injections may cause swelling and bruising. Most episodes are very mild and resolve shortly. DO NOT APPLY cosmetics or creams to the site of ANY injection for at least 12 hours:
    •    To reduce chances of bruising, please avoid the following for 72 hours prior to your visit: any form of alcohol, aspirin, medications containing ibuprofen, aspirin, and naproxen, CoQ10 and fish oil supplements.
    •    To aid in faster recovery, you can take sublingual Arnica or use the topical gel prior to and after the procedure. These can be purchased at most health stores.

PRE TREATMENT INSTRUCTIONS FOR DERMAL FILLERS:
    •    To aid in faster recovery, you can take sublingual Arnica or use the topical gel prior to and after the procedure. These can be purchased at most health stores.
    •    To reduce chances of bruising, please avoid the following for 72 hours prior to your visit: any form of alcohol, aspirin, medications containing ibuprofen, aspirin, and naproxen, CoQ10 and fish oil supplements.

POST TREATMENT INSTRUCTIONS FOR DERMAL FILLERS:
    •    Avoid blood thinner medication for 3 days 
    •    Avoid alcohol for 3 days 
    •    If experiencing swelling, sleep with head elevated for 2-3 days to decrease swelling.
    •    You may take acetaminophen/Tylenol if you experience any mild tenderness or discomfort.
    •    Wait a minimum of two weeks before receiving any skin care or laser treatments.
    •    Expect bruising swelling so plan this around social calendars; Full effect and tissue integration takes effect in 2-3 weeks. 
    •    To help minimize bruising, Arnica Montana natural supplement can be taken to help bruising resolve at a faster rate; take 5 tablets three times per day (this can be started 1 week prior to injections). Arnica products can be purchased at health stores.
    •    DO NOT APPLY ANYTHING TO THE SKIN UNTIL THE DAY AFTER TREATMENT: No cleanser, moisturizer, or makeup
    •    DO NOT massage, touch, or manipulate the injection site. 
    •    Ice packs may be used to the treated area during the first 12 hours. Ice for 15 minutes on every hour.
    •    Avoid heavy exercise the day of your treatment.
    •    Avoid dental work for 2 weeks after filler injections.
    •    Avoid laying or sleeping face down, on your side or or on your stomach for 7-10 days post-treatment to prevent dispersing the product. We recommend you sleep elevated on your back and use pillows to stabilize yourself. 
* If you notice onset of any of these following symptoms, please alert our office immediately. 
    •    Sharp pain
    •    Headache
    •    Increasing warmth or coolness to touch
    •    Unusual bruise or spreading bruise
    •    Skin changes (severe swelling or redness)
